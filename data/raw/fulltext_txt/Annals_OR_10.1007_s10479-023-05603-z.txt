The Editor-in-Chief and the publisher have retracted this article. The article was submitted to
be part of a guest-edited issue. An investigation by the publisher found a number of articles,
including this one, with a number of concerns, including but not limited to compromised
editorial handling and peer review process, inappropriate or irrelevant references or not
being in scope of the journal or guest-edited issue. Based on the investigation’s ﬁndings
the Editor-in-Chief therefore no longer has conﬁdence in the results and conclusions of this
article.
All authors disagree with this retraction.
The online version of this article contains the full text of the retracted article as Supple-
mentary Information.
